During my tenure at Apollo Bank I had the privilege of working on the online banking e-mail campaigns. These campaigns reached over 3,000 users. I learned to code like it’s 1999, the importance of in-lining CSS and using tables for layout structures, not assuming that all images will be rendered and always remember to include a plain text version.
